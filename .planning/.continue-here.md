<decisions_made>

v2.3.12 decisions:
- trackGain() follows Spotty pattern: suppress LMS Default Adjustment when normalization active
- PCM gapless: no rate-limiter reset needed (audio is continuous)
- OGG gapless: continuous cumulative timeline (ogg_track_base_frames + ogg_timeline_frames)
- OGG headers: per-page buffering (not whole chunks) for rapid-skip safety
- granule_offset = 0 (not -1) at serial change to count first audio page frames
- Header detection: granule <= 0 (not == 0) to handle continuation pages

PlayPlay research findings:
- Spotify migrating ALL formats (including OGG) from Shannon/AP to PlayPlay — per account cohort
- New accounts (post ~2020) get AP RequestKey denied — librespot cannot stream
- go-librespot has PlayPlay plugin architecture but ships empty stub (DMCA risk)
- Rust librespot has zero PlayPlay support, maintainers say they cannot pursue it (Spotify contacted them)
- DMCA enforced: SpotifyDL (Apr 2025), EeveeSpotify + 520 forks (Aug 2025)
- No public timeline for Shannon deprecation, but direction is one-way
</decisions_made>
