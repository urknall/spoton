<objective>
Implement per-player crash-loop protection with auto-reset cooldown, LMS-start reset, per-player Discovery toggle in Settings UI, and the supporting prefs/strings infrastructure.

Purpose: The current crash-loop protection sets a global `disableDiscovery` flag permanently (never auto-resets). One player with Discovery issues disables Discovery for ALL players. Users must manually intervene to restore Discovery. This plan fixes all three problems: (1) per-player scope, (2) 30-minute cooldown auto-reset via timer, (3) LMS-restart reset as backup, plus (4) a user-facing Discovery toggle per player.

Output: Complete Perl-side infrastructure for per-player Discovery management with crash-loop resilience.
</objective>
