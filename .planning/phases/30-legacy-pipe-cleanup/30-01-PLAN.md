<objective>
Delete the four legacy Perl daemon modules (Browse::DaemonManager, Browse::Daemon,
Connect::DaemonManager, Connect::Daemon), remove all dual-path gates from Plugin.pm and
ProtocolHandler.pm, strip Connect.pm's init() shim, remove son-* entries from
custom-convert.conf, and remove dead standalone entry points from main.rs / browse.rs /
connect.rs.

Purpose: Phase 29 shipped the unified daemon and passed UAT. The legacy modules
and their gate code are now dead weight — 1379 LOC of Perl plus Mode::Browse,
Mode::Connect, and Mode::SingleTrack in Rust. Removing them eliminates confusion,
prevents accidental reactivation, and shrinks the maintenance surface.

Output:
- 4 Perl files deleted
- Plugin.pm simplified (unconditional unified startup)
- ProtocolHandler.pm simplified (unified-only dispatch)
- Connect.pm simplified (init() removed)
- custom-convert.conf: son-* entries removed, soc pcm * * retained
- main.rs: 3 dead Mode variants removed, run_single_track() removed
- browse.rs: run_browse() + browse_http_server() removed; BrowseHttpSink + serve_track_request() kept
- connect.rs: run_connect() + http_stream_server() removed; LMS struct + HttpStreamSink kept
</objective>
